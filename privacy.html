PRIVACY POLICY

Welcome to the official website for Scripture Says. We value your privacy and want to be clear about how this website operates.


Effective Date: 29th May 2026

1. WHO WE ARE

Scripture Says Acappella is a music group using this website to share content, promote music, and provide links to external platforms.

2. PERSONAL DATA WE COLLECT

We do not actively collect personal data from visitors to this website. You can browse our site without:

  • Creating an account
  • Subscribing to a newsletter
  • Providing personal details such as your name, email address, or phone number

3. INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY

When you visit this website, certain limited technical data may be collected automatically by our hosting provider. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of access

This information:

  • Does not identify you personally
  • Is used only for website functionality, security, and performance monitoring

4. COOKIES

We do not use cookies for tracking or marketing purposes. However:

  • Basic, essential cookies may be used by our hosting provider to ensure the website functions properly
  • Third-party platforms (see below) may use their own cookies if you interact with their content

5. THIRD PARTY LINKS AND SERVICES

Our website includes links to third-party platforms such as:

  • Spotify
  • YouTube
  • Amazon Music
  • Social Media Platforms

If you click on these links, you will be directed to external websites. We do not control how these platforms collect or use your data. We recommend reviewing their individual privacy policies.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our content is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect or process personal data from children.

7. DATA SECURITY

Although we do not collect personal data, we take reasonable steps to ensure the website is secure and protected against unauthorised access or misuse.

8. YOUR RIGHTS (UK GDPR)

Under UK data protection law, you have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing

Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights will generally not apply through this website. However, if you believe otherwise, you may contact us.

9. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

10. CONTACT

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: scripturesaysacappella@gmail.com
